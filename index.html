About Me

Hello! I'm Ahmed, a passionate AI and Machine Learning Engineer with a deep-seated curiosity for creating intelligent systems that solve real-world problems and expand the limits of AI. My journey into AI began with a fascination for how data can be used to train AI to do almost anything, leading me to specialize in deep learning, particularly in the realms of Computer Vision and NLP. I thrive on the entire development lifecycle, from conceptualizing models and processing vast datasets to deploying robust, scalable applications on cloud platforms like AWS.

My project experience, from building complex MRI segmentation models with hybrid architectures to developing predictive demand systems and recommendation engines, reflects my commitment to hands-on learning and practical application. I am driven by the challenge of pushing the boundaries of what's possible and am always exploring new techniques, like model size reduction and building classifiers from the ground up, to drive innovation and creativity in the field of artificial intelligence.
